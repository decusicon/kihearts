About KiHearts

KiHearts Network is a donation and reward based crowdfunding platform for kind hearted people, connected globally to render financial and non-financial help to one another.

It's a social community of like minded individuals, those who want to help others financially so as well for others to help them financially whenever they need financial help; coming together to support each other financially through generous free will donations.

The financial structure of this world has not been fair to all, nearly 70% of the world's population struggles financially to make ends meet, many live below the poverty line, and most times goes to bed hungry. KiHearts Network was created to give financial relief to those group of people by uniting them under one network to pull financial resources together in order to raise funds for one another according to their financial needs.

KiHearts Network is a fund mine which gives every members the opportunity to generate money through her crowdfunding program, investment program and her remarkable affiliate program.

KiHearts Network would be positined in a way that every week; she would be sharing between 100 Million Naira -- 1 Billion Naira every week amongst her members.

KiHearts Network is here to stay and had been programmed to continue rendering her services over the next 10 decades.

MISSION

Our mission is to give financial relief to those who struggles financially to make ends meet, as we seek to raise funds for over 1 Billion people across the Globe.

VISION

We are focused to be at the peak of the crowdfunding industry as we aspire to be number 1 in crowdfunding services in less than 10 years.
