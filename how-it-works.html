How it works

Kihearts Network renders four services namely: Donation and Reward Crowdfunding, Investment, Loan and Advertising.

There is a 4 steps registration process which covers all the services rendered by Kihearts:

Step 1: Collects your personal data plus your preferred login username and password.

Step 2: Collects your fund request

Step 3: Is where you make a one time registration payment of $1.50₵ or ₦500.

Step 4: Is where you make pledge. The minimum pledge amount is ₦100 or $1 and the maximum pledge amount is ₦20,000 or $200.

Now let's see how each services works:

CROWDFUNDING

After completion of the 4 registration steps, your fund campaign becomes Active but if you've skipped the registration fee (step 3), your fund campaign will be inactive pending when you've paid.

Every fund campaign goes through 3 stages before funds campaigned for are paid. These stages are: the Active stage, the Published stage and the Selected stage.

💠The Active Stage

Your fund campaign becomes active immediately after you complete the four steps registration process. At the active stage, you can edit your fund campaign information but when its published, you can't edit anymore.

At this stage, you are expected to generate 20,000 Gold Coins (GC) before you could be able to publish your fund campaign for selection and funding. Below are ways to generate Gold Coin (GC) and the amount of Gold Coin (GC) for each way:

(a) For successful completion of the registration process, you get 1,000 GC.

(b) For making pledge you get 100 GC pending till you fulfill the pledge. That is for every pledge you make, you get 100 GC each but they all remain delayed till you fulfill all. If you fulfill 3 out of 5 pledges you made, you get rewarded for 3 pledges fulfilled.

(c) For fulfilling your pledge, you get rewarded according to the amount you donated. The higher the amount donated, the higher the Gold Coin reward. Minimum donation amount is $1 or ₦100, GC reward is 100 and maximum donation amount is $200 or ₦20,000, GC reward is 2,000.

NOTE: You can make multiple pledges. When pledges are made, the pledge info box becomes Green. When the system selects you to fulfill your pledge, a 3 days timer and a fulfill pledge button appears on the pledge info box, click the fulfill pledge button to make donation. The system gives you 3 days to fulfill your pledge. After fulfilling your pledge, the pledge info box becomes Golden and then the system rewards you with Gold Coin according to the amount donated and your pending GC for making the pledge will reflect in your Gold Coin Wallet.

(d) For each daily login (once), you get 100 GC

(e) For promoting Kihearts on social medias with your affiliate link, you get 50 GC.

(f) For every new member joining Kihearts through your affiliate link, you get 250 GC.

(g) For every new member you sent welcome message, you get 5 GC.

(h) For your testimony:

Video testimony, you get 1,000 GC and plain text testimony, you get 500 GC.



When you have successfully gathered 20,000 GC, a "Publish" button will appear on your fund campaign info box. Click the publish button to get your fund campaign published for possible selection and funding.

💠 The Published Stage

At this stage, the system selects campaigns starting from the least requested amount first and then moves to the higher ones.

The system selects all funds campaigns starting from the least fund amount first and when it gets to a total fund amount equal to or a little less than the total pledge amount, it stops selecting until new pledge amounts reaches the set Benchmark amount to enable the system resume selection again.

For emergency cases, there is an exemption, the system selects the first 5 emergency cases before it begins the above selection process of taking the least fund campaign amount first.

💠 The Selected Stage

Its at this stage that fund campaigns selected from the published stage are funded with the total amount of fulfilled pledges. It takes roughly 3-5 working days to transfer all funds to their respective campaign owners.

NOTE: 5% commission fee and recommitment fee is deducted from every successful fund campaign. That is 2.5% for commission and 2.5% for recommitment.

The Crowdfunding services runs on a two e-wallet system - Gold Coin e-wallet and Cash e-wallet.

💰Gold Coin E-wallet: This e-wallet is where all the Gold Coin earned by members are held. When yours reaches 20,000 GC, you can use it to publish your fund campaign. Also, at any amount of GC earned, you can decide to sell your GC at this fixed price range - $0.01₵ - $0.05₵ or ₦1 - ₦5. The money paid to purchase your Gold Coin goes into your cash e-wallet and its ready for withdrawal at anytime.

💰Cash E-wallet: The cash e-wallet is the main wallet as it holds all cash earned from the following: sell of Gold Coin, Crowdfunding, Investment and affiliate commissions.

Affiliate Commission for the Crowdfunding service is 50% of the recommitment fee of 2.5% deducted from the fund campaign amount of the person you referred to the Kihearts Network. The other 50% is rolled back into the system to help other members.

NOTE: Referral is not compulsory as the Kihearts program is not a Multi Level Marketing (MLM) program, it is not Networking as some of you think. But if you want to make money from the system apart from the money you get from your fund campaign, referring is the only way.
